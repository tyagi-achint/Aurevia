Shipping Policy

At Aurevia, we understand that the presentation and arrival of a gift are as important as the gift itself. Every collection is packed with meticulous care to ensure it reaches its destination in pristine condition.

Domestic Delivery

Complimentary standard shipping on all curated collections within the continental US. White-glove delivery is available for bespoke installations.

International Shipping

We offer carbon-neutral international shipping to over 40 countries. Duties and taxes are calculated at checkout for transparency.

  • Orders for stock collections are processed within 48 hours.
  • Bespoke and corporate orders follow a custom timeline discussed during consultation.
  • Real-time tracking is provided via email once the order departs our atelier.

Returns & Replacement

Due to the artisanal and personalized nature of our products, our return policy is designed to maintain the highest quality of service.

“Luxury is found in the details, and your satisfaction is our primary metric of success.”

Curated Collections

Items from our standard collections may be returned within 14 days of receipt, provided they are in original, unopened packaging. A restocking fee of 15% applies.

Bespoke & Personalized Items

Customized gifts, engraved items, and bespoke boxes are final sale. However, if an item arrives damaged or incorrect, we will prioritize an immediate replacement at no cost.
