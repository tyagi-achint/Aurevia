Common Enquiries

Find answers to our most frequent concierge queries below.

Can we customize individual items? expand_more

Absolutely. We offer bespoke branding on individual products, from hot-stamping leather goods to custom-etched glassware. Our design team will work with you to ensure your brand's narrative is seamlessly integrated.

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)? expand_more

For standard collections, our MOQ starts at 25 units. For fully bespoke designs that require custom sourcing and packaging, we typically suggest an MOQ of 50 units to maintain the highest standard of craftsmanship.

How long does delivery take? expand_more

Standard curated boxes ship within 7–10 business days. Custom-branded orders typically require 3–4 weeks for production and quality checks. We provide tracked courier services for all corporate shipments across major metro cities.

Are your prices inclusive of GST? expand_more

Our quotes are transparent and itemized. GST is calculated at the standard rate of 12% or 18% depending on the specific goods within the curated selection. All corporate inquiries receive a formal proforma invoice with GST details.
